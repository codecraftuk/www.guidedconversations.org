Questions by Irina Preda in September 2018

  • What does it mean to be inclusive?
  • What are examples of ways spaces can exclude others?
  • Why is diversity important?
  • What are some examples when we felt particularly included or excluded?
  • What was done differently/ could have been done differently?
  • How can we enhance our understanding of others' experiences and needs?
  • What can holds up back in our work towards being inclusive?
  • How do we put the ideas we've gained in practice?
  • When do we say something is inclusive (how do we evaluate it)?
  • How do we create a continuous process of learning?
  • What will we do outside of this meeting to create more welcoming spaces?

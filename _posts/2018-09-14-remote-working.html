Remote Working

Questions by Nigel Runnels-Moss in September 2018

  • How many people here are currently working Remote, either full- or part-time?
  • Why are you interested in Remote?
  • How would you characterise a Remote organisation, as opposed to conventional or other orgs?
  • What makes Remote work different to on-site work?
  • Are there different kinds of Remote work? What are they like?
  • How does a team being Remote affect their collaboration?
  • What tools do you think are indicated or contraindicated by Remote?
  • What processes do you think are indicated or contraindicated by Remote?
  • Do Remote organisations need a different structure to conventional orgs?
  • If you were to found your own Remote organisation, what would be most important to you?
